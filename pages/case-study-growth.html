Business Growth

How Wellabe Accelerated Growth with MyEnroller

Client Profile

Wellabe — a mutual insurance organization serving agents and policyholders across multiple supplemental insurance products, focused on sustainable growth and disciplined underwriting.

The Challenge

As Wellabe pursued aggressive growth objectives, it faced a familiar challenge: how to move faster without increasing operational strain or underwriting risk. Product updates, underwriting refinements, and distribution changes were increasingly important — but traditional workflows made those changes slow to implement. Each new product launch or adjustment required coordination across multiple systems, lengthy development queues, and careful change management. To sustain momentum, Wellabe needed a way to move faster at the front end of the buying process — without sacrificing control or quality.

The Solution

Wellabe deployed MyEnroller — a configurable buying and underwriting platform designed by carrier operators to support rapid iteration at the point of sale. Rather than treating enrollment as a static workflow, MyEnroller gave Wellabe the ability to introduce product and underwriting changes more quickly, adapt eligibility, workflows, and decisioning without large system rebuilds, reduce friction for agents while maintaining underwriting guardrails, and gain faster feedback from real market behavior. By shifting more decisioning and configuration closer to the buying moment, Wellabe was able to compress development cycles and respond to opportunities as they emerged.

The Impact

  • More rapid product iteration — Teams were able to make meaningful product and workflow changes far more frequently than before.
  • Shortened launch timelines — New initiatives moved from concept to market in a fraction of the time, enabling Wellabe to capitalize on demand more quickly.
  • Year-over-year sales more than doubled — Improved speed and agent experience translated into significantly higher production.
  • Sustainable execution at scale — Growth was achieved without adding complexity or increasing operational risk.

The Takeaway

By using MyEnroller to accelerate time to market and reduce friction at the point of sale, Wellabe transformed growth from an aspiration into execution — demonstrating how carrier-designed platforms can unlock speed, flexibility, and measurable results.
