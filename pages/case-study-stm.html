Accelerating Time to Market

How Pylaex Orchestrator helped a carrier cut new product launch cycles from months to weeks.

Time to Market

The Challenge

A regional life and health carrier was spending 4–6 months on every new product launch — waiting on IT to update underwriting rules, configure form logic, and test enrollment flows. By the time a product launched, the market window had often narrowed.

The Solution

Pylaex Orchestrator replaced the IT-dependent rules-change process with a carrier-controlled configuration studio. Business users now own product setup, rules, and form logic. New products are configured in sandbox, QA'd, and promoted to production on a business-team timeline. The first product launched in 18 days.
