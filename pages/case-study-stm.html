Time to Market

Accelerating Time to Market with FXe Orchestrator

Client Profile

Mid-sized regional insurance carrier — multi-product carrier serving a national distribution footprint with a focus on simplified underwriting and agent-centric sales.

The Challenge

Like many carriers, this organization faced increasing pressure to respond faster to market opportunities without overburdening internal IT and operations teams. Traditional enrollment and underwriting workflows made even modest product changes costly and time-consuming. Each update required extended development cycles, coordination across multiple systems, and careful risk review — slowing the carrier's ability to test, learn, and adapt in-market. The result: missed opportunities, delayed launches, and reduced agility at a time when speed mattered most.

The Solution

The carrier partnered with FXe — a configurable buying and underwriting platform built by former carrier operators who understand the realities of insurance operations. FXe was implemented as the system of engagement for new business, enabling the carrier to configure products and underwriting workflows without rebuilding core systems, make changes closer to the point of sale rather than deep in downstream processes, gain real-time visibility into submission activity and outcomes, and reduce reliance on long IT release cycles for routine product updates. Rather than treating speed and risk management as tradeoffs, the platform was designed to support both — allowing the carrier to move faster within clear underwriting guardrails.

The Impact

  • More frequent product enhancements — Teams could introduce new variations, eligibility refinements, and workflow changes without lengthy development backlogs.
  • Shorter launch cycles — What previously took months could be accomplished in weeks — or faster — allowing the carrier to respond quickly to distribution and market feedback.
  • Increased experimentation — Because changes were easier to implement and reverse, the carrier became more willing to test improvements, learn from real results, and iterate.
  • Year-over-year growth more than doubled — Increased speed and flexibility translated into stronger sales performance for early adopters.

The Takeaway

By adopting a carrier-designed buying platform, this organization transformed time to market from a bottleneck into a competitive advantage — enabling faster launches, more iteration, and sustained growth without increasing operational risk.
