Preneed

Preneed, Modernized for Consistency and Quality

Preneed insurance presents unique operational challenges — paper applications, inconsistent submissions, and limited automation have historically slowed growth and constrained underwriting outcomes. MyEnroller was built to change that.

📄→💻

Transform a Paper-Driven Market into a Digital One

MyEnroller enables carriers to move Preneed out of legacy, paper-based workflows and into a fully electronic, production-ready environment using application scanning and intelligent intake.

  • Ingest paper applications digitally without changing how agents sell
  • Normalize data capture across agents and markets
  • Moved carriers from ~20% to 100% electronic submissions
⚙️

Increase Straight-Through Processing by Design

MyEnroller improves Preneed underwriting by embedding underwriting logic directly into the application flow — enabling cleaner submissions and earlier risk assessment.

  • Cleaner, more complete submissions
  • Earlier risk assessment
  • Straight-through processing from essentially 0% to over 60%
✅

Improve Quality Through Consistency, Not Friction

Quality of business improves when underwriting intent is applied consistently across every agent and every submission.

  • Standardized workflows that reduce variability
  • Guided applications that prevent common errors
  • Uniform rule execution across all channels
🤝

Support Innovation Without Disrupting Distribution

Preneed distribution often depends on long-standing agent and funeral home relationships. MyEnroller respects that dynamic while still enabling innovation — making modernization additive, not disruptive.

  • Incremental modernization — no forced wholesale change
  • Scanning and guided workflows layer on top of existing relationships
  • Innovation becomes a growth enabler, not an operational disruption

Built for the Reality of Preneed

MyEnroller's Preneed capabilities weren't adapted from another product — they were designed deliberately for the realities of the market. By combining digital intake, underwriting automation, and carrier-defined controls, MyEnroller turns Preneed into a scalable, repeatable, and high-quality growth engine.
