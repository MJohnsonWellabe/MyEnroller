The Challenge

A final expense carrier experienced higher-than-expected claims in 24-month cohorts. Analysis revealed agents were skipping reflexive underwriting questions on paper apps because the process made it easy to. The problem wasn't fraud — it was friction in the underwriting conversation.

The Solution

Pylaex Orchestrator's adaptive questionnaire engine fires reflexive questions automatically — agents cannot proceed without answering. The carrier configured health indicator triggers in the rules studio without code changes. Within two enrollment cycles, reflexive question completion was 100%.
