What the Demo Shows

From Intake to Issued Policy in One Sitting

⚡

Real-Time Qualification

The platform evaluates eligibility at the point of sale — surfacing what the applicant qualifies for before moving to product selection. No back-and-forth with underwriting.

🎯

One Application, Multiple Products

Shared intake data flows across all selected products. Agents enter information once and the platform routes it appropriately across Medicare Supplement, Cancer, Final Expense, and more.

✅

Instant Issuance

Qualified applicants receive policy numbers immediately at the point of sale. The experience ends with confirmation — not a promise to follow up.

🔒

Carrier-Defined Guardrails

Every decision in this flow reflects configurable carrier rules. The platform enforces underwriting intent automatically — the agent simply guides the applicant through.
