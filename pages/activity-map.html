Apptelligent Pulse

See Your Business in Motion

Apptelligent Pulse provides a real-time visual view of business activity as it happens — bringing transparency to production, engagement, and momentum across products and geographies.

Rather than waiting for reports or aggregated summaries, carriers and teams can see their business moving in real time — making it easier to identify opportunities, respond to trends, and stay ahead of the market.

  • Real-time visual production monitoring by geography
  • Instant feedback loops to inform product and underwriting decisions
  • Transparency across agents, channels, and products
  • Identify momentum and opportunities as they emerge
