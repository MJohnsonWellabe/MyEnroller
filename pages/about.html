The People Behind the Platform

Meet the Team

MJ
Chief Executive Officer

Matt Johnson

Matt Johnson is a career insurance operator with deep experience spanning financial reporting, product strategy, and enterprise growth. Prior to founding MyEnroller, Matt served as Chief Actuary and Growth Officer at a multi-product insurance carrier, with responsibility across Medicare Supplement, supplemental health, and life markets.

During his tenure, Matt helped scale Medicare Supplement sales from under $20 million to more than $180 million in annual production in just five years, while modernizing underwriting, distribution, and platform capabilities. He is known for blending rigorous actuarial discipline with a builder's mindset, leading complex transformations that connect technology, risk, and growth.

At MyEnroller, Matt brings a carrier-first perspective to building platforms that actually work in production — designed by people who understand the realities of insurance, not just the theory.

BB
Lead Developer

Bill Butters

Bill Butters is the principal architect behind the MyEnroller platform. With decades of experience solving carrier challenges through technology, Bill has built systems that sit at the intersection of underwriting, operations, and distribution.

At his prior carrier, Bill led the design and development of a modern buying and underwriting platform that became a material driver of growth — helping enable rapid iteration, improved quality of business, and dramatically higher sales volume. His work directly supported the carrier's ability to scale while maintaining underwriting discipline.

Bill brings a rare combination of deep technical expertise and real-world carrier experience, ensuring MyEnroller is designed for speed, configurability, and production-grade reliability.

DK
Strategic Advisor

Dave Keith

Dave Keith serves as a Strategic Advisor to MyEnroller, bringing decades of executive leadership and platform-building experience in the insurance industry.

Earlier in his career, Dave co-founded SE2 and helped build the company from the ground up into a leading annuity administration and outsourcing platform. He has extensive experience launching and scaling technology-enabled insurance businesses, working closely with carriers, distributors, and partners across the annuity and life markets.

As an advisor, Dave provides strategic guidance on platform commercialization, operating model design, and long-term growth — drawing on a career spent building durable insurance infrastructure.

TN
Operating Advisor

Todd Nevenhoven is an Operating Advisor to MyEnroller with deep experience leading operations at both large carriers and third-party administrators (TPAs).

Throughout his career, Todd has built, scaled, and modernized operational teams supporting underwriting, policy administration, and customer service across multiple insurance lines. He has worked extensively with TPAs and carrier operations to improve efficiency, accountability, and execution at scale.

At MyEnroller, Todd brings an operator's lens to platform implementation and client delivery — ensuring growth is supported by operational models that work in the real world.
