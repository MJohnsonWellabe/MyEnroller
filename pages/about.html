About Pylaex

Built by career insurance operators who spent years inside carriers.

Why we built this

Every major carrier has hundreds of engineers, decades of proprietary technology investment, and platforms that took billions to build. The regional carrier, the preneed specialist, the final expense challenger — they've been competing with the same handcuffs on for years. The technology gap between large carriers and everyone else isn't a talent problem. It's an access problem. We built Pylaex to close it.

Pylaex was founded by career carrier operators who understand the real constraints of underwriting, distribution, and operations. Our first client more than doubled sales year over year by removing friction at the point of sale and making better decisions earlier in the buying process.

"Level the field. Own the market."

The Team

Built by Carriers, for Carriers

MJ
Chief Executive Officer

Matt Johnson

Matt Johnson is a career insurance operator with deep experience spanning financial reporting, product strategy, and enterprise growth. He served as Chief Actuary and VP of Strategy & Platform Enablement at a mid-size carrier, where he owned platform strategy, capital allocation, and board-level communications.

Prior to founding Pylaex, Matt spent years watching carriers lose market share not because of their people or their products, but because their technology couldn't keep up. At Pylaex, Matt brings an operator's perspective to every product and go-to-market decision — ensuring the platform is built around how carriers actually work.

BB
Chief Technology Officer

Bill Butters

Bill Butters is the lead developer and technical architect of Pylaex. With decades of experience solving carrier challenges through technology, Bill has built systems that sit at the intersection of underwriting, operations, and distribution.

At his prior carrier, Bill led the design and development of a modern buying and underwriting platform that became a material driver of growth — enabling rapid iteration, improved quality of business, and dramatically higher sales volume. Bill brings a rare combination of deep technical expertise and real-world carrier experience, ensuring Pylaex Orchestrator is designed for speed, configurability, and production-grade reliability.

DK
Strategic Advisor

Dave Keith

Dave Keith serves as a Strategic Advisor to Pylaex, bringing decades of executive leadership and platform-building experience in the insurance industry.

Earlier in his career, Dave co-founded SE2 and helped build the company from the ground up into a leading annuity administration and outsourcing platform. He has extensive experience launching and scaling technology-enabled insurance businesses, working closely with carriers, distributors, and partners across the annuity and life markets. As an advisor, Dave provides strategic guidance on platform commercialization, operating model design, and long-term growth.

TN
Operating Advisor

Todd Nevenhoven

Todd Nevenhoven is an Operating Advisor to Pylaex with deep experience leading operations at both large carriers and third-party administrators (TPAs).

Throughout his career, Todd has built, scaled, and modernized operational teams supporting underwriting, policy administration, and customer service across multiple insurance lines. At Pylaex, Todd brings an operator's lens to platform implementation and client delivery — ensuring growth is supported by operational models that work in the real world.

Brand Identity

The Pylaex Brand

Pylaex comes from pylae — the gates of Thermopylae, where a small, disciplined force held off a vastly larger one — and -ex, Latin for force issuing outward. The name signals what the platform does: it levels the field and lets that force out.
