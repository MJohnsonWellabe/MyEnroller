Agent Satisfaction

The Challenge

An ancillary health carrier saw elevated agent churn on its independent channel — not from commission or product issues, but because the enrollment process was slow, error-prone, and frustrating. Exit surveys consistently cited "technology."

The Solution

Pylaex Orchestrator replaced the legacy platform with a clean guided experience that worked on mobile, returned real-time decisions, and gave agents a single view of pipeline, pending cases, and approvals. First-year agent retention improved significantly in the first Pylaex cohort.
