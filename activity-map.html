What FXe Pulse Delivers

Pulse aggregates data from FXe Orchestrator and Enroller to give leadership a real-time picture of what's happening across distribution — not a report from last week.

  • Live activity map showing carrier footprint and agent activity by geography
  • Agent engagement metrics, production scoring, and activity tracking
  • Placement rate trends by product, channel, and agent segment
  • Pipeline health — submissions in progress, pending, and issued
  • Straight-through processing ratios and underwriting outcome analytics
  • Configurable dashboards for executive, operations, and distribution teams
  • Alert thresholds — flag unusual activity patterns before they become problems

The Advantage

Most carriers are operating on reports that are days or weeks old. By the time a problem shows up in the data, it's already cost you production. Pulse changes that — when something shifts, you see it in real time and can act before it becomes a trend.
