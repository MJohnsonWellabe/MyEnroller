Who We Are

Purpose-Built for Modern Insurance

Pylaex is a carrier platform company. We give small and mid-size carriers the technology the big carriers built over decades.

Our Mission

Pylaex's mission is to empower small and mid-size carriers with technology that, until now, has only existed within the largest insurers in the world. Completely configurable, rapidly deployed, and built to help clients grow faster with better outcomes.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the trusted platform partner for modern insurance buying and underwriting — recognized for speed, flexibility, and results — while reshaping how carriers go to market in a digital-first world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Pylaex is a carrier platform company. Our suite — Orchestrator, Enroller, Pulse, Engage, and Launchpad — gives small and mid-size carriers the technology the big carriers built over decades. Real-time underwriting decisions. Straight-through processing. Analytics that show exactly how you're competing. Configured to how you operate, deployed in weeks.

Pylaex is purpose-built for small and mid-size insurance carriers — the disruptors, the up-and-comers, the carriers who compete on capability instead of scale. If your technology is holding you back from the speed, quality, and agent experience your competitors have, that's exactly who we built this for.

Yes — completely. Pylaex Orchestrator is the configurability engine. Your underwriting philosophy, your rules, your product structure. You decide what decisions are made, when they're made, and how they're applied. Speed happens within your guardrails, not around them.

Pylaex Enroller is the guided, intelligent interface agents use from first question to issued policy. It's the evolution of the traditional e-app platform — configurable, carrier-branded, and built to make agents faster and more effective in the field. The close is in the room. Enroller makes sure nothing gets in the way of it.

Pylaex Pulse is executive-level intelligence. Activity maps, agent engagement metrics, placement rate trends, pipeline health, and straight-through processing ratios — exactly how your carrier is competing, in real time. Built for the people who need to make decisions, not just watch dashboards.

Pylaex Launchpad is our carrier implementation system — configuration, training, and go-live infrastructure that takes carriers from signed contract to live production in weeks, not months. We've launched carriers before. We have the playbook. Configured for you means exactly that.

Pylaex Engage covers every touchpoint between carrier, agent, and applicant: agent messaging and communication tools, agent incentives (Spark), DTC capabilities, lead transfer and queue management (LeadFlow), intelligent product matching (MatchIQ), and application scanning (ScanIQ). One engagement suite, unified across the relationship.

We built this from inside a carrier. Our founders ran underwriting, distribution, and actuarial operations. We felt the exact pain this platform solves. The result is a system that reflects carrier reality — not a technology company's theory of what insurance should look like. We've been the carrier. We know what you need.
