Frequently Asked Questions

Pylaex Orchestrator is a configurable buying and underwriting platform built to help carriers move faster, convert more business, and make better decisions at the point of sale. It combines modern enrollment, real-time underwriting decisioning, and data visibility into a single, carrier-designed platform — without adding operational complexity.

Pylaex Orchestrator is built for carriers and distributors who want speed without sacrificing control. If you're running health or life products through an agent channel — individual, worksite, or direct — and you want a modern enrollment and decisioning experience without rebuilding your core systems, Pylaex is built for you.

Pylaex Orchestrator is API-first and integrates with your existing policy administration and billing systems. We have pre-built connectors for major PAS platforms and a standard REST API for everything else. No PAS replacement required.

Pylaex Orchestrator supports individual life and health products: Medicare Supplement, preneed life, final expense, hospital indemnity, individual term and whole life, annuities, and worksite/voluntary benefits.

Most single-product implementations go from signed contract to pilot launch in four to six weeks. Full platform rollouts typically run eight to twelve weeks. Every implementation is led by a dedicated implementation manager — not a rotating support queue.

Pylaex Engage360 is the suite of supplemental tools that work alongside Pylaex Orchestrator — including Pylaex Pulse, MatchIQ, Pylaex Link, Pylaex SPark, ScanIQ, and Pylaex LeadFlow. Each module addresses a specific friction point and can be deployed independently or as a bundle.
